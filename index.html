Egg Stew

Egg stew is bisically tomatoes stew with eggs in it

Recipe Information

It will take approximately 45 minutes for meal to be prepared.

preparation time:15mins

Cook time:30 minutes

Time:10 minutes

Number of servings:8

Difficulty Level:biginner.

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Onions
  • Powdered pepper
  • Smoked fish(salmon)
  • Eggs
  • Maggie

Note

Wash hands properly before beginning,Wash all vegetables with salt solution.

Instruction

  • Slice your fresh tomatoes and onions.

Step by step cooking directions

  1. Put your saucepan on fire and pour your oil into it.Allow the oil to be hot and pour part of your onions in the oil and add your blended ginger and garlic and allow to fry for 2-3 minutes.
  2. You now proceed to add your tomato paste,allow to fry for another 2-3 minutes then add your fresh tomatoes.allow to cook for 5-10 minutes under low to medium heat.
  3. Now go ahead and add your powdered pepper,maggie,smoked fish and salt.stair and allow to cook for 10-15 minutes.
  4. Your egg stew is ready,this could served with cooked rice,cooked yam,cooked plantain etc.
